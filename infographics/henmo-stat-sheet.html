SADI
Schedule-Adjusted
Dominance Index
// What it measures
How dominant a team is after accounting for the strength of its opponents.
// Interpretation
↑ HIGH Dominating strong teams
↓ LOW Dominance inflated by weak schedule
// Equation
Net Rating × (Opp Net Rating /
Natl Avg Opp Net Rating)
ODCI
Offense-Defense
Context Index
// What it measures
Whether a team's success comes more from offense or defense.
// Interpretation
POSITIVE Offense-driven team
NEGATIVE Defense-driven team
≈ ZERO Balanced team
// Equation
((Off Rtg × Opp Def Rtg) −
(Def Rtg × Opp Off Rtg)) / 10
TCES
Tempo-Controlled
Efficiency Score
// What it measures
Team efficiency per possession, removing pace impact so fast and slow teams can be compared fairly.
// Interpretation
↑ HIGH More efficient overall performance
// Equation
Net Rating × (Natl Avg Tempo /
Team Tempo)
BER
Balanced
Efficiency Ratio
// What it measures
How balanced a team is between offensive and defensive strength.
// Interpretation
BALANCED Complete team
↑ HIGH Offense-heavy team
↓ LOW Defense-heavy team
// Equation
Offensive Rating / Defensive Rating
